    About

    Hi there!

    I’m Adriana, a motivated UX/UI designer based in Zurich, Switzerland. I am passionate about creating great and useful user experiences and products that are adapted with pleasure by the users and that makes their life easier.

    I come from an interdisciplinary background with two Masters degrees: one in Landscape Architecture and one in Marketing and I worked for several years on the administrative business side in multinational organizations. As I am passionate about digital products, effective design and efficient interfaces that meet user’s needs, I have decided to change my career path to a more creative one and become a UX/UI designer. In this profession, I can use my creativity, problem solving and strong people skills.

    I am a friendly and positive person, that loves to spend the free time as much as possible in nature and with great people. Hiking, discovering new places, sports, photography, animals, nature, chocolate and the smile of the dear ones are the main things that usually make my day.
